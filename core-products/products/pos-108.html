POS-108

POS Family

POS108 is both a Dual Cash Drawer Alarm or a Printer Receipt Alarm all in one. Easy to install, No tools or software changes needed. Plug and Play. The POS108 can connect to virtually any POS Printers with a RJ12 Cash Drawer Port. The built in Dual Cash Drawer splitter offers an easy option to connecting two cash drawers to one POS printer. The drawer open alarm feature is easily configured offering a variety of user settings.
