K9-MAG-2DW-FP

K9 Family

K9 MAG-2DW-FP is the Ideal Multi-Functional Barcode ID, Magstripe and Fingerprint Reader all in one to help you Read 1D and 2D barcodes, Magnetic Stripe data from Drivers Licenses, Health ID Cards, Membership IDs and for two factor authentication fingerprint bio-metric reading. The K9 offers diversity and ease of use thru its innovative design. Two Barcode Auto trigger Sensors & a Manual trigger Button are built in as a standard feature of all the K9 versions. Optional RFID HF, RFID DF, and IC Smart Card readers are available as an add-on.
