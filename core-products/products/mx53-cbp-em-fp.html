MX53-CBP-EM-FP

MX53 Family

MX53-CBP-EM-FP is a compact, lightweight Design for Reading 125 KHz Low Frequency Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) cards, 3 tracks Magstripe data cards and a built-in Fingerprint Biometric-reader. The MX53-CBP-EM-FP is a 3 in 1 reader which offers diversity and ease of use thru its innovative design which can be easily installed vertically or horizontally. Enhance your login security with the 3 in 1, two factor Authentication device.
