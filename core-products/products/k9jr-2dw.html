K9Jr-2DW

K9Jr Family

K9Jr 2DW is the Ideal Multi-Functional Barcode ID Reader to help you Read 1D and 2D barcodes from Drivers Licenses, Health ID Cards and Membership IDs. The K9Jr offers diversity and ease of use thru its innovative design. Two Barcode Auto trigger Sensors & a Manual trigger Button are built in as a standard feature of the K9Jr. Optional RFID HF, RFID DF, IC Smart Card and Fingerprint readers are available as an add-on.
